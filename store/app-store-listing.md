## Description

**The word game that finally moves.**

A single lettered tile falls from the top of the board. You steer it left or right and drop it where you want. But instead of clearing rows, you're spelling words — and in LEXIS a word can read in EIGHT directions: across, down, and along both diagonals. A tile you place can finish words you didn't even plan.

Find them, clear them, and keep the board from filling. Simple to learn, endlessly deep.

**WHY IT'S DIFFERENT**
• Words read 8 ways — across, down, and both diagonals
• You steer the fall — tap to nudge, slide to fling, swipe down to drop. Real control, not luck.
• Side-sticking — catch the edge of a tower and build words at height
• Power-ups earned by playing well — bombs, tips, freeze, reroll, peek
• Glow-then-clear — words light up gold; a tap confirms the clear, so every find feels deliberate

**PLAY YOUR WAY**
• Daily Challenge — the same 40 letters for everyone, once a day. Build a streak.
• Weekly & Weekend events — a fresh seeded challenge every week on a shared leaderboard
• Endless — four speeds from Relaxed to Insane; chase your best
• Duel a friend — send a code, play the identical letters, compare scores

**KEEP COMING BACK**
• Level up, complete daily goals, and earn coins
• Collect tile themes, clear-burst effects, and board backdrops
• Game Center leaderboards and achievements
• A daily reminder so you never miss your streak

One letter. One word. One life. Download LEXIS and find the words hiding in the fall.
