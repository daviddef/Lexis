Privacy Policy

LEXIS · Last updated 8 July 2026

This policy explains what information the LEXIS mobile app ("LEXIS", "we") handles and why. LEXIS is a word-puzzle game. We aim to collect as little as possible.

Information we do not collect

LEXIS does not ask you to create an account, and does not collect your name, email, contacts, photos, or location. Your game progress — scores, streaks, level, coins, and settings — is stored on your device.

Game Center

If you sign in to Apple Game Center, your scores and achievements are submitted to Apple's leaderboards under your Game Center identity. This is handled by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy; we do not receive your Apple ID.

Advertising

LEXIS is free and supported by optional, rewarded ads — you only see an ad when you choose to watch one (for example, to earn coins or a power-up). We never show forced full-screen ads.

Ads are served by Google AdMob. To show ads, AdMob and its partners may access your device's advertising identifier and general device information. If you consent via the App Tracking Transparency prompt, ads may be personalized; if you decline, you'll see non-personalized ads instead. You can review or change this in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. See how Google uses information from apps that use its services.

Analytics

We may collect anonymous, aggregated gameplay events (such as which mode was played or when a run ended) to understand how LEXIS is used and improve it. These events contain no personal information and are not used to identify you.

Children

LEXIS is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Where ads are shown to users we cannot verify as adults, non-personalized ads are used.

Your choices

  • Decline ad tracking via the App Tracking Transparency prompt or iOS Settings.
  • Delete all local data at any time by deleting the app.
  • Sign out of Game Center to stop submitting scores.

Changes

We may update this policy as LEXIS evolves; the "last updated" date above will change accordingly.

Contact

Questions? Email hello@lexisgame.app.
