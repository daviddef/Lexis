Across, down, and along both diagonals. A tile you place can complete words in directions you didn't even plan.

Tap to nudge the falling letter, slide to fling it across the board, double-tap to drop. Real control, not luck.

Catch a tower's edge to build at height. Earn bombs, tips, freeze, reroll and peek by playing well.
